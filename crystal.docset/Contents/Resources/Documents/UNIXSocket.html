Overview

A local interprocess communication clientsocket.

Only available on UNIX and UNIX-like operating systems.

Example usage:

require "socket"

sock = UNIXSocket.new("/tmp/myapp.sock")
sock.puts "message"
response = sock.gets
sock.close
