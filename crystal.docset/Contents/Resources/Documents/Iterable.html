module Iterable

Overview

The Iterable mixin provides convenience methods to collection classes that provide an #each method that returns an Iterator over the collection.

Direct including types

  • Array(T)
  • Deque(T)
  • Dir
  • Range(B, E)
  • Set(T)
  • Slice(T)
  • Tuple(T*)

Defined in:
