Overview

DWARF Line Numbers parser. Supports DWARF versions 2, 3 and 4.

Usually located in the .debug_line section of ELF executables, or the __debug_line section of Mach-O files.

Documentation:

  • DWARF2 section 6.2
  • DWARF3 section 6.2
  • DWARF4 section 6.2
